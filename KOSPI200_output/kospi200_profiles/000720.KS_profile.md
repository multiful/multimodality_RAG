## 사업 개요
Hyundai Engineering & Construction Co.,Ltd. engages in the civil engineering and construction business in Middle Asia, Asia, Africa, and internationally. It operates through Building/Housing, Civil Engineering, Plant/Power, and Others segments. The company constructs roads and bridges, tunnels and railways, and maritime and port, apartment, luxury homes, and large complex as well as develops industrial facilities, mixed-use development and hotel, hospitals and offices, and special buildings. It is also involved in energy infrastructure construction, including large nuclear power plants, oil and gas, oil refining and petrochemicals, industrial plants, transmission lines and substations, renewable energy, and nuclear decommissioning. In addition, the company engages in steel structure manufacturing and installation; agricultural management and livestock industry; management of resort condominium; real estate development and supply; equipment maintenance; electricity production; construction of commercial building; business facility management service; and renting heavy equipment. The company was founded in 1947 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
