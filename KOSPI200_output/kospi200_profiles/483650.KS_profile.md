## 사업 개요
d'Alba Global Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacturing and sale of perfumes and cosmetic products in South Korea and internationally. The company offers mists, sun care, serums, creams, masks, and sunscreens under the d'Alba brand; health functional foods under the Veganery brand name; health beauty devices; and low-irritation beauty solutions. It sells its products through B2C, home shopping, B2B customers, offline distribution channels, as well as online. The company also owns mall. The company was founded in 2016 and is based in Seoul South Korea.
