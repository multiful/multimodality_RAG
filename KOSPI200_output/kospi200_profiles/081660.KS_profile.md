## 사업 개요
Misto Holdings Corp., together with its subsidiaries, engages in the sale of textile products, clothing, footwear, leather products, watches, cosmetics, golf equipment, and other products under the FILA brand name in Korea and internationally. The company manufactures and sells golf products, including golf balls, clubs, and gears under the Titleist brand; and licenses FILA branded products. It also exports its products. Misto Holdings Corp. was formerly known as FILA Holdings Corporation and changed its name to Misto Holdings Corp. in April 2025. Misto Holdings Corp. was founded in 1991 and is based in Seoul, South Korea.
