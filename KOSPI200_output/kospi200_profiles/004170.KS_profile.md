## 사업 개요
SHINSEGAE Inc. operates department stores in South Korea. The company operates through Department Store, Wholesale and Retail Industry, Real Estate Industry, Terminal industry, Hospitality, Duty-Free Store, and Others segments. It also manufactures and retails clothing and furniture; manufactures, retails, and distributes cosmetics; develops and leases real estate properties and vehicle terminal; offers advisory and online retail services; and operates duty-free stores. In addition, the company engages in the information and communication, trading, broadcast program production and supply; financial investment, and hotel businesses. SHINSEGAE Inc. was founded in 1955 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
