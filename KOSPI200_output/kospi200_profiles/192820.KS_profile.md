## 사업 개요
Cosmax, Inc. researches, develops, produces, and manufactures cosmetic and health function food products in Korea and internationally. The company operates through Cosmetic Business segment, Custom Cosmetic, and other Business segments. The company offers skincare products under the Capsule+, IS-LCE, CeraPresso, Emulixir, Foamology, IRPT, and Solar Clear brands; base-make up under the Air Cloud, and skin fabric names; and make up under the Xfine, JellTeX, ARTCOTTA, Bi-Coat, creamline, Thixolash, and CRYSTAL PRISM brands; oriental medicine under the Ginsenology name; and fragrance under the Scenteritage brand, as well as hair care products. The company was formerly known as Miroto Korea and changed its name to Cosmax, Inc. in 1994. Cosmax, Inc. was founded in 1992 and is headquartered in Hwaseong-si, South Korea.
