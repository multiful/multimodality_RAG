## 사업 개요
Daesang Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, manufacture seasonings and food additives products in Korea, Asia, the United States, Europe, Oceania, and Africa. It operates through Food and Material segments. The company offers general food products, including bean pastes and seasonings; agro-fishery food, such as vinegar and fish sauce; and fresh food products comprising Kimchi and tofu, western food, deli, and frozen food. It also provides food ingredients, such as starch used for food, paper, textile, and construction materials; and sweeteners used in alcohol drinks, beverages, confectionery, baking, and other industrial areas. In addition, the company offers bio-ingredients, including flavor enhancers, amino acids, and microalgae-based products used in food, dietary supplements, functional food, animal nutrition, pet food, and pharmaceutical industries. Further, it engages in trade and real estate activities. The company also exports various bio-ingredient products to approximately 80 countries under the Miwon brand. Daesang Corporation was incorporated in 1956 and is based in Seoul, South Korea.
