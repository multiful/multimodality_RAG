## 사업 개요
Kangwon Land, Inc. engages in the casino, tourist hotel, and ski resorts businesses in South Korea. Its business also covers condominium, golf course, and a water park. The company operates the Kangwon Land Casino with 200 game tables, 1,360 slot machines, and video games. It also operates the Kangwonland Hotel, Convention Hotel, High1 Hotel, Mountain Condominium, Valley Condominium, and Hill Condo; High1 Ski Resort; High1 CC public golf course; and a gambling addiction care center, as well as High1 sports teams. Kangwon Land, Inc. was founded in 1994 and is based in Jeongseon-eup, South Korea.
