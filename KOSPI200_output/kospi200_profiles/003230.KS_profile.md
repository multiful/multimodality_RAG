## 사업 개요
Samyang Foods Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, manufactures and sells food products in South Korea and internationally. It offers noodles, snacks, sauces, seasonings, dairy products, and frozen food under the Samyang 1963, Samyang Ramyun, Buldak, Map Tank, Pulse Lab, Jjajjaroni, and Tangled brand names. The company also engages in cardboard processing and sale; vegetable soup selection and packaging; grain manufacturing; livestock farming; other processed food wholesale; warehousing and transportation; dealership; and investment activities. It exports its products. The company was founded in 1961 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
