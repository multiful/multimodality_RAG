## 사업 개요
KRAFTON, Inc. engages in the software development and related ancillary businesses in Asia, Korea, the United States, Europe, and internationally. The company offers PC, mobile, console, and other games. It is also involved in development and distribution of mobile games and applications, and game software business; consulting advisory and investment activities; production of video contents; and development and servicing of games. The company was formerly known as Bluehole Studio Inc. and changed its name to KRAFTON, Inc. in November 2018. KRAFTON, Inc. was incorporated in 2007 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
