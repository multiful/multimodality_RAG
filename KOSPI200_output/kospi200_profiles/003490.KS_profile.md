## 사업 개요
Korean Airlines Co.,Ltd. provides airline services. It operates through Air Transport, Aerospace, Hotel, and Others segments. The company provides passenger and cargo transportation services and maintenance of aircraft and manufacturing of aircraft parts. It also engages in hotel, hotel management, accommodation, software development and supply, aircraft engine repair, sports and leisure services, cargo information network service, online sales, aircraft transport services, information technology services, airport support services, online sales, facilities support management services, and small aircraft transport service. Additionally, the company offers cargo information network services; and supplies labors. It operates a fleet of 162 aircraft connecting 10 domestic and 106 international destinations in 39 countries. Korean Airlines Co.,Ltd. was founded in 1962 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
