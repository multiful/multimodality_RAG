## 사업 개요
APR Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells cosmetic products for men and women. The company also offers medical cosmetics and diet products. In addition, it provides electronic beauty products; and clothes through its online store. Additionally, the company operates an instant photo booth. It sells its products under the APRILSKIN, medicube, GLAM.D Bio, Forment, and NDY brand names. APR Co., Ltd. was founded in 2014 and is based in Seoul, South Korea.
