## 사업 개요
LS ELECTRIC Co., Ltd. provides smart energy solutions in South Korea and internationally. The company offers smart power solutions, including energy management systems, supervisory control and data acquisition systems, distribution management systems, distribution control systems, micro grid systems, and diagnosis solutions; DC, AC, and EC-charger solutions; and LSPS, which performs automatic selection of breakers and determination of protection coordination through fault calculation. It also provides low and medium voltage, dc component, and protection/measurement/meter devices; and power transmission, power distribution, modular substation, and photovoltaic systems, as well as modular scalable platform and energy storage systems. In addition, the company offers smart automation solutions, such as precision plenary gearbox, PLC, servo/motion, HMI, and AC drive (VFD) solution; and smart railway solutions comprising power supply and signaling systems. The company was formerly known as LSIS Co., Ltd. and changed its name to LS ELECTRIC Co., Ltd. in 2020. LS ELECTRIC Co., Ltd. was founded in 1974 and is headquartered in Anyang-Si, South Korea.
