## 사업 개요
F&F Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells apparel and accessories in South Korea. The company offers fashion products under the MLB, MLB KIDS, DISCOVERY, DUVETICA, SUPRA, and SERGIO TACCHINI brands; and skincare products under the BANILA CO brand. It also retails its products through fashion specialty outlet. The company was founded in 1992 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
