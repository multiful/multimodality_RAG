## 사업 개요
Hanwha Engine Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells diesel engines worldwide. The company provides low and medium speed marine diesel engines for ocean-going vessels; and diesel/gas engines for electric power generation. It also offers engine parts, including cylinder liners, piston crowns, exhaust valve spindles and housings, pump barrel plungers, piston rings, spindle guides, exhaust valve seats, puncture valves, and main bearings; and warranty, operation and maintenance, technical, installation and commissioning, and preventive services. Hanwha Engine Co., Ltd. was founded in 1983 and is headquartered in Changwon-si, South Korea.
