## 사업 개요
Samsung Electro-Mechanics Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells various electronic components in Korea, China, Southeast Asia, Japan, the Americas, and Europe. The company operates through three segments: Component, Package Solution, and Optics Solution. It offers multilayer ceramic capacitors, inductors, tantalums, and chip resistors; camera modules; package substrates; and semiconductor PKG boards. The company is also involved in the real estate and real estate software, as well as investment business. Its products are used in automotive, computer, display, mobile phone, network, server, solid state drive, tablet, and wearable products applications. The company was formerly known as Samsung Electronic Parts Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Samsung Electro-Mechanics Co., Ltd. in February 1987. The company was incorporated in 1973 and is headquartered in Suwon-si, South Korea.
