## 사업 개요
L&F Co., Ltd. engages in the development and sale of electronic materials in Korea. It offers cathode active materials used in IT devices, household appliances, and power tools, as well as electric vehicles and power storage devices. The company also provides LiNiMnCoO2, LiCoO2, and LiMn2O4 products. In addition, the company offers wireless TFT LCD, and PMMA plates and sheets. L&F Co., Ltd. was founded in 2000 and is headquartered in Daegu, South Korea.
