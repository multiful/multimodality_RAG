## 사업 개요
ORION Holdings Corp. manufactures and sells confectioneries in South Korea, China, and internationally. The company operates through Confectioneries, Entertainment, Construction, and Others segments. It provides pies, chips, cookies, fruit juices, cakes, chocolates, baked breads, jellies, energy bars, protein drinks, protein balls, brownies, cheese chips, potato snacks, mineral water, nutritional supplements, biscuits, snacks, and rice crackers. The company offers its products under the Choco Pie, Kkobuk Chips, Poca chips, The Taste of the Sun! Sun', Cuttlefish Peanut Ball, Oh!Gamja, Mutuktuk Potato Chips & Mutuktuk Sweet Potato Chips, Goraebab, Custard, Diget, Mygummy, BICHOBI, Marie Biscuit, Tok Ping, Market O Brownie, Market O Potato Tok & Market O Squid Tok, Dr. You, Hao Li You Pai, Ya! Tudou, Hao You Qu, Shu Yuan, Hao Duo Yu, Q Di, Mo Gu Li, Guo Zi Guo Zi & Guo Zi Guo Xin, Lang Li Ge Lang, Songsong Lowsong Dangao, An, C'est Bon, O'Star & Swing, Custas, Masita, Goraebab & Jungle Boy, Goute & Marika & de Marie, BOOM Jelly, Chop Chop, Choco Boy, Tender and Soft Choco Chip Cookie, CRACK IT, Goute, Mr. Bagetti, O'rice, and Turtle Chips brands. It also offers convenience meal replacement products under the Market O Nature, O!Granola, O!Granola Bar, O!Granola Cacao, and O!Granola Pop brands. In addition, the company invests in and distributes films; and constructs and sells real estate projects; and engages in beverages, and investment and trading businesses, as well as provides business information services. It also exports its products to approximately 60 countries, including China, Vietnam, Russia, India, the Americas, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. The company was formerly known as Orion Corporation and changed its name to ORION Holdings Corp. in June 2017. ORION Holdings Corp. was founded in 1956 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
