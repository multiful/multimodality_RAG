## 사업 개요
Hyundai Elevator Co., Ltd. designs, manufactures, installs, maintains, and modernizes elevators in South Korea and internationally. The company offers ultra-high speed elevators under THE EL name; high-speed elevators under the i-XEL name; and low and mid-speed elevators under the LUXEN, YZER, and N:EX names, as well as observation, freight, automobile, and marine elevators. It also provides escalators and moving walks, parking systems, and advanced remote maintenance systems. The company was incorporated in 1984 and is headquartered in Chungju-si, South Korea.
