## 사업 개요
CJ Logistics Corporation provides transportation and logistics services in Korea and internationally. The company engages in Ecommerce logistics, parcel, contract logistics, forwarding, project logistics, stevedoring, and transportation businesses. CJ Logistics Corporation was formerly known as Korea Express Co., Ltd. The company was founded in 1930 and is based in Seoul, South Korea. CJ Logistics Corporation operates as a subsidiary of CJ Corporation.
