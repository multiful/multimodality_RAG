## 사업 개요
HD Construction Equipment Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacturing and distribution of construction equipment. The company offers excavators, wheel loaders, backhoe loaders, skid steer loaders, compact track loaders, special equipment, articulated dump tracks, wide-body dump tracks, dozers, and motor graders, as well as parts and accessories. It operates under the HYUNDAI and DEVELON brand names. The company was formerly known as HD Hyundai Construction Equipment Co., Ltd. and changed its name to HD Construction Equipment Co., Ltd. in January 2026. HD Construction Equipment Co., Ltd. was founded in 1985 and is based in Seoul, South Korea.
