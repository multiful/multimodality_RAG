## 사업 개요
Dong Suh Companies Inc. engages in the food, packaging, tea, logistics, and import and export businesses. The company produces, imports, and sells food, such as palm oils, cheese, processed fruit and vegetable products, and processed grain products; packaging materials, such as flexible packaging films for food; and tea products, such as coffee, brown rice green tea, and roundworm tea, as well as desserts. Dong Suh Companies Inc. was founded in 1975 and is based in Seoul, South Korea.
