## 사업 개요
Ecopro Materials Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, produces precursor materials for nickel-based anode materials for secondary batteries in South Korea. Its products are used in power tools, electric vehicles, e-bikes, V/C, drones, and electric energy storage systems. The company also develops technology to eliminate and prevent various air pollutants; and engages in the lithium-ion, filter frames, industrial gas production, and battery recycling businesses. Ecopro Materials Co., Ltd. was founded in 2017 and is headquartered in Pohang, South Korea.
