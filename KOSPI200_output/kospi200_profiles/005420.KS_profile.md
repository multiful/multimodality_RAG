## 사업 개요
Cosmo Chemical Co., Ltd., a chemical materials company, manufactures and sells titanium dioxide (TiO2) anatase in South Korea. The company offers COTIOX KA-100, a general purpose TiO2 anatase product for use in various applications, including rubbers, paints, papers, resins, etc.; and COTIOX KA-300, a TiO2 anatase product used in textiles comprising polyester filament, stable, and acryl fibers, as well as for use in plastics, inks, etc. It also provides ferrous sulfate heptahydrate for use in waste treatment agents, other raw materials, and catalysts; poly ferric sulfate solution, which is used as a waste treatment agent for dyeing, leather, paper, foods, and other wastes; cobalt sulfate liquid and cobalt sulfate solid phase for use in lithium secondary batteries, paint drying materials, cobalt pigments, tire bonds, and others; and gypsum used in construction materials. In addition, the company offers textiles, food, and electronic products. The company was founded in 1968 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
