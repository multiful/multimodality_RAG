## 사업 개요
LOTTE Fine Chemical Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells chemical products in South Korea, the rest of Asia, North America, Europe, and internationally. The company offers chlorine relatives, including epichlorohydrin, caustic soda, tetramethyl ammonium chloride, 3-Chloro-2-Hydroxypropyl trimethylammonium chloride, methyl chloride, methylene chloride, methaclean, chloroform, sodium hypochlorite, and hydrochloric acid; ammonia relatives comprising urea solution, ammonia, monomethylamine, dimethylamine, trimethylamine, and trimethylamine-HCL; and toners for printers. It provides green materials, such as cellulose products, under the MECELLOSE, HECELLOSE, AnyCoat, and AnyAddy brand names. LOTTE Fine Chemical Co., Ltd. was formerly known as SAMSUNG Fine Chemicals Co., Ltd. and changed its name to LOTTE Fine Chemical Co., Ltd. in 2016. LOTTE Fine Chemical Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1964 and is based in Ulsan, South Korea.
