## 사업 개요
Young Poong Corporation operates as a non-ferrous metal smelting company in Korea. The company offers high purity zinc ingots that are used in hot dip galvanizing, die casting alloys, electroplating and sacrificial anodes, photo engraving plates, zinc oxide, and zinc plates, as well as zinc jumbo bars for hot dip galvanizing; combination zinc bars for continuous and hot dip galvanizing; and zinc alloy ingots for use in automobiles, electric devices, toys and leisure goods, home appliances, accessories, office equipment, and communication equipment, as well as zippers, industrial machinery and tools, builders' and domestic hardware, plastic and press molds, and gears. It also provides sulfuric acid for inorganic chemicals and metal refining, as well as steel, spinning and weaving, chemical and fertilizer, fiber, and paper manufacturing industries; copper sulfate for agrichemicals, reagents, electroplating, and metallurgy; electrolytic copper cathode for brass and bronze, plates, and rods and pipes; silver-by product for photographic materials, sterling and electroplated ware, catalysts, and jewelry. The company was founded in 1949 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
