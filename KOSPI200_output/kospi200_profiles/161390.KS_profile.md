## 사업 개요
Hankook Tire & Technology Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells tires in South Korea and internationally. It offers tires for electric vehicles, passenger cars, SUVs, light trucks, buses, and trucks under the iON, Ventus, Dynapro, Weatherflex, Kinergy, Winter i*cept & Winter i*pike, Vantra, Smart brands. The company was formerly known as Hankook Tire Co., Ltd. and changed its name to Hankook Tire & Technology Co., Ltd. in May 2019. Hankook Tire & Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 1941 and is headquartered in Seongnam-si, South Korea.
