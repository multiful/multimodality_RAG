## 사업 개요
Taihan Cable & Solution Co., Ltd. manufactures, processes, and sells electric wires, cables, and related products worldwide. The company offers power generation products, such as transmission cables, distribution cables, industrial cables, heating systems, and busducts; renewable energy cables, comprising submarine and solar power cables; communication cables, including LAN, coaxial, railway, local communication, UL style, CATV, and other cables; and optical cables, which consists of outdoor and indoor optical cables, OPGW/photoelectric composite, conductors, and accessories, as well as base metal products that include copper rod and wires and magnet wires. It also provides power transmission and distribution; offshore wind power; communication; plants; mobility; residential and commercial buildings; vessels and ports; and information and communications technologies solutions. In addition, the company involves in construction and real estate investment business. The company was formerly known as Taihan Electric Wire Co., Ltd. Taihan Cable & Solution Co., Ltd. was founded in 1941 and is headquartered in Anyang-Si, South Korea.
