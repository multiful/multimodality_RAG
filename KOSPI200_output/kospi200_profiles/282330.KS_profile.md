## 사업 개요
BGF Retail Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, operates convenience stores under the CU brand in South Korea. The company's stores retail various products, such as quick meals, instant cooking, processed meals, food ingredients, beverages, dairy products, alcohol, confectionery, ice cream, breads, desserts, snacks, tobacco, household and personal goods, hobbies and leisure products, securities and service products, and other products. It is also involved in the sale of products to franchisees through the convenience store chain business; logistics services; advertising, E-commerce, and convenience store delivery; food manufacturing; and oil business. BGF Retail Co., Ltd. was founded in 1990 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
